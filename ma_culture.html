Influence on Culture.

She had many influences in her style from Madonna, Micheal Jackson, and Janet Jackson. She emulated Madonna’s fashion and started wearing more revealing outfits in her concert. She was a fashion designer she created all her outfits herself. She created her own line of work and opened up two boutiques in 1994 called Selena Ect. with a beauty salon inside of it.

She influenced so many people to this day people like Demi Levato, Selena Gomez, Beyonce, Whitney Houston, Lady Gaga, Marc Anthony, Ivy Queen, Katy Perry, Daddy Yankee, Aventura, Angelo Garcia, Jenni Rivera, Becky G, Ashley Simpson, Meghan Trainor, Paulina Rubio, Drake, Gloria Estefan, Jeniffer Lopez, Romeo Santos, Cardi B, and so many more.

She still has many fans to this day to the point where fans on her 20th anniversary of her death did a petition in 2015 to have MAC makeup company create a collection to commemorate her. The collection called Selena La Reina was released in 2016 and it sold out immediately. Again, it’s up for grabs in April, 2020. This is to commemorate her death of twenty-five years.
